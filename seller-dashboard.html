Seller Rules & Guidelines

account_balance_wallet Revenue Generation & Tracking

To ensure complete transparency between sellers and the platform, your Total Earnings are only calculated based on completed transactions.

Important: You must update the status of an order to "Delivered" for the system to recognize it as a successful sale and add it to your dashboard earnings and platform cards.

percent Platform Fees Visibility

The platform fee is automatically deducted from every successfully delivered order. For clarity, you can view the admin amount generated (Platform Fee) on:

  • The main dashboard (Platform Fees Deducted total).
  • Each individual customer order card (labeled as Fee).
  • The detailed order breakdown when you click on any specific order.

touch_app Mobile Quick Navigation

Quick actions: Simply tap or long-press the floating button to instantly reveal options like contacting Support or Logging out.
