password 4. Password Best Practices

A strong password is your first line of defence. We strongly recommend following these practices to protect your Kwabz Store account:

  1. Use at least 10 characters — the longer, the harder to crack.
  2. Mix character types — combine uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols (e.g. K@mpus#2026!).
  3. Avoid obvious choices — never use your name, birthday, phone number, "password", or "123456".
  4. Never reuse passwords — if another service you use is breached, a unique password keeps your Kwabz account safe.
  5. Use a password manager — tools like Bitwarden or 1Password generate and store strong passwords securely so you don't have to remember them.
  6. Enable biometrics — where available, use fingerprint or face recognition for quick and secure sign-in on your personal device.
Tip: When you sign up, Kwabz Store shows a real-time password strength indicator. Aim for a "Strong" rating before submitting.

security 5. Platform Security

Kwabz Store implements multiple layers of security to protect your data and transactions:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. Look for the padlock icon in your browser's address bar as confirmation.
  • Firebase Authentication: Passwords are never stored in plain text. Firebase hashes credentials using bcrypt before storage — even we cannot read your password.
  • Firestore Security Rules: Access to your personal data (orders, address, profile) is locked to your authenticated user ID only. Other users cannot query your records.
  • Biometric Sign-In: Supported devices can use Touch ID, Face ID, or Windows Hello via the WebAuthn standard for passwordless login.
  • Session Management: Sessions expire after inactivity. Sensitive operations require re-authentication to prevent unauthorized access.

Despite these measures, no system can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to report any suspected vulnerability to support@kwabzstore.com.
